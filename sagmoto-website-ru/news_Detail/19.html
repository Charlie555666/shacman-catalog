Поздравление SAGMOTO с Китайским Новым годом 2025

As we step into a new year, the future shines brightly. On the occasion of the Spring Festival, I, on behalf of all the staff and leadership of SAGMOTO, extend our heartfelt greetings and best wishes for the New Year to our valued friends in government, throughout the industrial chain, among our customers, and across society who have supported and cared for our development throughout this year.

Looking back at the past year, all employees have held high the flag, kept the instructions in mind, and thoroughly implemented the spirit of the 20th National Congress of the Communist Party of China and the second and third plenary sessions of the 20th Central Committee. We have solidly carried out party building and party discipline education, fully and accurately implemented the new development philosophy, led the development of new types of productivity with scientific and technological innovation, and used deepening reforms as a driving force. We achieved sales of 167,000 units of various types of vehicles, successfully completing the annual targets and tasks. The "2035 Strategy" and the 14th Five-Year Plan of the enterprise have made new progress and achievements, with a strong momentum towards high-quality development.Все the way through wind and rain, all the way forward, all the way to harvest. These achievements lie in the scientific guidance of Xi Jinping's thought of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era, thanks to the strong support of leaders at all levels and friends from all walks of life, but also the majority of cadres and workers to spell out, do, and strive. In the main battlefield of sales, the first line of production, the forefront of service, leaving a frame of unforgettable images, here, on behalf of SAGMOTO, I would like to express my heartfelt thanks to all of you!

The year 2025 is the final year of the national “14th Five-Year Plan” and the planning year of the “15th Five-Year Plan”, and it is also the key year for the enterprise to practice the “innovation-driven, pragmatic, cooperative and empowering, new-quality development”. We should continue to take Xi Jinping's thought of socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new era and the spirit of the “Four New” instructions as our guide, promote the enterprise's transformation and upgrading with high-quality party building, increase the long-term strategic layout, and ignite a new engine for the enterprise's new quality productivity to build up new kinetic energy.

Throughout the journey of thousands of miles, our original aspiration remains unchanged. At the forefront of the tide, we will unite our efforts, strive fiercely, and forge ahead, braving the winds and waves as we work together to create an even brighter future for SAGMOTO!

Warmest wishes for a prosperous and thriving motherland, with spring-like harmony and tranquility!

May SAGMOTO continue to flourish and thrive! 

 

Chairman of Shaanxi Automobile Holdings Group Co., Ltd.

Yuan Hongming
