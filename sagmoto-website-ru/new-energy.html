Электромобили Applications

SAGMOTO is committed to sustainable transportation with our expanding range of new energy commercial vehicles. From pure electric cargo trucks to advanced hybrid solutions, our zero-emission vehicles deliver the same reliability and performance you expect from SAGMOTO, while significantly reducing environmental impact and operating costs. Perfect for urban logistics, municipal services, and green construction projects.

Urban Green Logistics

SAGMOTO electric cargo trucks are purpose-built for urban delivery and logistics operations. With zero tailpipe emissions and whisper-quiet operation, these vehicles are ideal for city centers with emission restrictions and noise-sensitive areas. Fast-charging capability and extended battery range ensure your fleet stays productive throughout the day without compromising on environmental responsibility.

  • Zero tailpipe emissions for urban low-emission zones compliance
  • Fast-charging technology with extended battery range up to 300km
  • Whisper-quiet operation ideal for nighttime deliveries
  • Significantly lower operating costs compared to diesel equivalents

Коммунальный & Sanitation Services

SAGMOTO electric special vehicles are transforming municipal operations worldwide. From electric sanitation trucks to clean-energy road sweepers, our municipal solutions combine zero emissions with powerful performance. Electric powertrains provide instant torque for stop-and-go operations, while regenerative braking recovers energy during frequent deceleration, maximizing efficiency in urban service routes.

  • Instant electric torque for efficient stop-and-go municipal operations
  • Regenerative braking recovers energy during frequent deceleration
  • Zero noise pollution for residential area operations
  • Compatible with solar charging stations and smart grid integration

Green Construction

SAGMOTO electric dump trucks bring zero-emission performance to construction sites. With no diesel exhaust, these vehicles improve air quality for workers and enable operation in enclosed or partially covered construction environments. The instant torque of electric motors provides excellent hauling capability, while the simplified drivetrain reduces maintenance requirements and downtime compared to conventional diesel trucks.

  • Zero emissions for safe operation in enclosed construction environments
  • Instant torque for excellent hauling and grade climbing capability
  • Simplified drivetrain with fewer maintenance points and lower TCO
  • Meets LEED and green building certification requirements
