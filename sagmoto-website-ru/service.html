To thank your support for SAG Commercial Vehicle Company, we provide you with an overseas comprehensive warranty policy. Extra long warranty period with unlimited mileage: 24 months for tractor, 18 months for cargo truck, 12 months for dump truck, 18 months for special vehicles. Fast and flexible after-sales appointments, 24-hour standby. If your vehicle experiences an irreparable breakdown during operation, do not hesitate, dial our local service hotline immediately, and we will promptly arrive on-site to assist you with the repairs.

We engage in close collaboration with overseas partners, utilizing a unique support model that combines overseas channel reserves with precision components pre-preparing. This effectively enhances the availability of after-services. Furthermore, based on your vehicle holdings, we can formulate personalized components assurance plans, ensuring you can get maintenance resources more quickly and conveniently.
