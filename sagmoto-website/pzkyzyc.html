Off-road Dump Truck

Greater carrying capacity · Higher attendance

Off-road mining dump truck is custom-built for roadless/off-road/mining conditions, with strong carrying capacity, high reliability, low failure rate, and flexible and convenient matching.

The new offset cab has been upgraded

The new offset cab has been upgraded, with increased internal space and upgraded exterior and interior design, resulting in improved driving comfort.

Product Advantages

Metal bumper is convenient to repair

 

Reinforced taillight protection grill with good wrapping performance, avoiding the taillight being damaged by falling sand and stones when the cargo is lifted.

 

With low intake resistance, the oil-bath air filter is adapted to dusty and sandy working environment.  The maintenance cost is also low.

 

(Optional) Reinforced oil sump protection grille, 6mm thick and 755mm wide, protects the water tank and oil sump. Better able to cope with poor road conditions.

 

850 wide structure: stable performance, good formation and assmebly processability, adaptable to harsh road conditions.

 

Triple beam construction: outer beams run from the front suspension to the rear of the frame.

 

Adjustable exhaust tailpipes can avoid the downward exhaust blowing up dust and affecting the view of the vehicle behind.

 

Tyre bolt strength grade 12.9, sufficient preload to cope with off-road conditions
