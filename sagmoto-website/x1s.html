X1s Dump Truck Series

Built for Latin America's toughest mining and construction environments, the X1s dump truck series combines Cummins engine reliability, MAN-technology axles, and a comprehensive factory protection package. Available in 6x4 (50T GVW) and 8x4 (90T GVW) configurations, the X1s delivers exceptional value at 40-60% less than equivalent European trucks.

Key Features

Cummins Engine — Global Support Network

Choose between Cummins ISME420 (420HP, 10.8L) for the 6x4 or Cummins M13E3 550 (520HP, 12.5L) for the 8x4. Both backed by the extensive Cummins service network across Latin America.

MAN-Technology HDZ16T Axles

Double-stage cast widened axles with 5.262 ratio — engineered for extreme torque multiplication on steep grades and soft terrain.

360° Surround-View Camera — Standard

Complete bird's-eye visibility eliminates blind spots during loading, unloading, and maneuvering. A premium safety feature included at no extra cost.

Complete Factory Protection Package

Headlight grilles, tail light grilles, water tank guard, oil pan guard, fuel tank anti-theft, battery anti-theft — all standard from the factory.

Mining-Reinforced Suspension (8x4)

14/14/12 four-main-leaf with four U-bolts — heavy-duty mining specification for sustained 90-ton operation in off-road conditions.

Latin America-Ready: Oil-Bath Air Filter

Superior dust filtration for dry, dusty mining and construction environments — far more effective than standard dry-element filters.

Frequently Asked Questions About X1s Trucks

What engine powers the X1s dump truck?

The SAGMOTO X1s runs on Cummins engines: ISME420 (420HP, 10.8L) with JACOB engine brake for 6x4, and M13E3 550 (520HP, 12.5L) for 8x4. The tractor variant uses the Cummins C520 (520HP). All backed by the global Cummins service network across Latin America, Africa, and the Middle East.

How much does the X1s cost?

FOB China pricing starts from ~$38,000 USD for the 6x4 dump truck, $43,000-$55,000 for the 8x4 mining configuration, and ~$38,000 for the 6x4 tractor. Final price depends on customization, order quantity, and destination. Request a quote for your specific requirements.

Why choose X1s over European trucks?

The X1s delivers 40-60% cost savings vs. comparable European mining trucks, while offering the same core components: Cummins engines, MAN-technology axles, 360° camera (standard), oil-bath air filter, and complete factory protection — all included at base price.

What configurations are available?

Three variants: 6x4 Dump Truck (50T, 420HP), 8x4 Dump Truck (90T, 520HP, mining suspension), and 6x4 Tractor Truck (520HP, high-roof sleeper with fold-down bed, heavy-duty fifth wheel).

Which markets do you serve?

We export to 50+ countries: Latin America (Peru, Chile, Mexico, Colombia, Argentina, Brazil...), Africa (Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, South Africa, Tanzania, Congo...), Middle East (Saudi Arabia, UAE, Iraq...), Southeast Asia (Philippines, Vietnam, Indonesia...), and Central Asia.

Is spare parts support available?

Yes. As an authorized SAGMOTO export dealer, Shaanxi Fenghan Trading provides genuine OEM parts, technical documentation, and after-sales service. Cummins engines are additionally covered by the global Cummins service network.
