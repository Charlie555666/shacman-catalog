Intelligent Production of Heavy Trucks at Shaanxi Automobile's Factory

2025-05-20

The intelligent production facility for heavy trucks with alternative drive systems, operated by Shaanxi Automobile Holding Group Co., Ltd. in the Jingkai District of Xi’an, is a prime example of advanced manufacturing expertise.

"This production line can assemble multiple models simultaneously and has an annual capacity of 50,000 heavy truck chassis and 100,000 cabs," explained Ji Xiang, deputy manager of Shaanxi Automobile’s general assembly plant.
