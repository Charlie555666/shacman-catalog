Caucasus' New Jewel SAGMOTO X3s Tractor Debuts in Armenia

2025-04-15

April 18-20, 2025, SAG INTL made a striking appearance at the Leasing Expo, a key machinery and equipment trade fair in the Caucasus region held in Armenia, showcasing its all-new X3s tractor and X9 cargo truck.

Situated in the southern South Caucasus with an average altitude of 2,000 meters, Armenia’s rugged terrain features predominantly mountainous winding roads. How to stand out in a market saturated with European brands? SAG INTL prioritized the production. Through extensive customer research and bench marking against mainstream models, the company launched localized and customized model - the X3s tractor, tailored for Armenian conditions. Key adaptive configurations include:

- Cummins 490 high-power engine for robust performance.

- Eaton automatic transmission for smooth operation.

- Voith retarder for enhanced safety on steep slopes.

This combination meets local demands for advanced configurations, load capacity, driving comfort, and smart features, attracting crowds of clients for test drives and inquiries during the exhibition.

Consistently upholding its mission of "Making favorite and reliable trucks". SAG INTL continues to deepen its understanding of user needs, drive technological innovation, and elevate product performance to deliver exceptional value for global customers.
