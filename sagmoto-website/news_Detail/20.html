SAGMOTO brand specialized trucks makes an appearance in the 137th Canton Fair

2025-04-10

The first phase of the 137th China Import and Export Fair concluded in Guangzhou on April 19. During the five-day exhibition, SAG INTL showcased its technological prowess in special purpose trucks. The highlights included the all-new i9pro electric truck, i9lec electric cargo truck, and X9 integrated sweeping vehicle, which covers both diesel and battery power sources. SAG demonstrates the company's comprehensive capabilities and confidence in global market expansion.

Adhering to its philosophy of "Making favorite and reliable trucks", SAG INTL has developed a full range of commercial vehicle star products by precisely addressing customers’ needs, delivering high-quality, high-value and high-level service product solutions with premium services worldwide.

The i9 series of electric trucks apply advanced technology such as large-capacity battery, electric drive axle, and power controller.

The following features make i9 stand out among its peers.

-400km comprehensive range enabled by high-capacity batteries

-Optimized power output across scenarios through intelligent algorithm-controlled electric drive axles

-Advanced power management system ensuring stable performance

Addressing traditional pain points in special purpose trucks like inconsistent modification standards and unstable performance, the company launched its branded special vehicle series featuring:

-Dedicated chassis design with integrated upper body.

-Comprehensive CAE analysis and testing verification for both chassis and upper body, aiming at optimal system matching.

The exhibited X9 integrated sweeper combines road cleaning and washing functions, adaptable to diverse operational requirements.

Looking ahead, SAG INTL will continue to focus on new energy and special purpose truck sectors, enriching product portfolios across segments while maintaining customer-centered innovation to provide globally favourite and reliable trucks.
