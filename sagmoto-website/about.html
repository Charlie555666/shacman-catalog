Who We Are

Dual-driven — Creating a new brand for the international market

Company Profile

Founded in 1968, Shaanxi Automobile Holding Group Company currently employs 32,000 staff with total assets of 34.8 billion CNY.

SAG Commercial Vehicle Company, a core subsidiary of Shaanxi Automobile Holding Group, locates in Baoji City, Shaanxi Province. Over the past decade, we have been dedicating to expanding our international business. It has successfully exported over 200,000 vehicles to more than 50 countries and regions worldwide.

SAGMOTO is a new brand initiative by Shaanxi Automobile Holding Group aimed at the international market. SAGMOTO provides customized commercial vehicle solutions, meeting the diverse needs of local markets worldwide.

SAGMOTO makes favorite & reliable trucks for consumers.

When We Started

Heritage since 1968

1968

Foundation

Chinese First Machinery Ministry established the Shaanxi Automobile Factory.

1978

National Award

Shaanxi Automobile Group Company's product, Yan'an SX250 SUV, won the National Science Congress Award.

1983

Steyr Project

Introduction of 'the Steyr Project', which changed the situation of trucks for civilian use in China.

2004

MAN Partnership

Shaanxi Automobile Group Company and MAN (Germany) signed an agreement on the introduction of MAN heavy truck technology.

2005

Cummins JV

Shaanxi Automobile and Cummins signed a joint venture contract in Xi'an to manufacture Cummins ISM 11L engines.

2008

10,000+ Vehicles

Shaanxi Automobile became the first enterprise in Shaanxi Province with production and sale exceeding 10,000 heavy trucks.

2009

National Tech Center

Nationally recognised enterprise technology centre and post-doctoral research station established.

2019

SAGMOTO Launch

The launching ceremony of SAGMOTO first product was held in SAG Commercial Vehicle Company's Baoji Industrial Park.
