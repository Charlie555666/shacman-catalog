Who We Are

SAGMOTO is a premier commercial vehicle brand operating under Shaanxi Automobile Group Co., Ltd., one of China's leading heavy-duty truck manufacturers. With a rich heritage dating back to 1968, SAGMOTO has evolved into a comprehensive commercial vehicle manufacturer dedicated to delivering high-quality, reliable, and cost-effective transportation solutions to customers worldwide.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing base is located in Baoji City, Shaanxi Province, occupying a vast industrial complex equipped with advanced production lines, automated welding systems, precision machining centers, and comprehensive quality testing facilities. We operate 7 production bases across China, enabling us to serve diverse market needs with localized manufacturing excellence.

SAGMOTO's product portfolio spans the complete range of commercial vehicles: from light-duty trucks for urban logistics to heavy-duty tractors for long-haul transportation, from rugged dump trucks for mining to specialized vehicles for municipal applications, and from reliable mixer trucks for construction to innovative new energy electric vehicles for sustainable transport.

Our Journey

Key milestones in SAGMOTO's history

1968

Foundation of Shaanxi Automobile Group

The parent company was established, laying the groundwork for what would become one of China's premier heavy-duty truck manufacturers.

2005

SAGMOTO Brand Launch

Launch of the SAGMOTO commercial vehicle brand, focusing on medium and heavy-duty trucks for domestic and international markets.

2010

Baoji Manufacturing Base Expansion

Major expansion of the Baoji Industrial Park with new production lines, increasing annual capacity to 100,000 units.

2015

Global Market Expansion

Export operations expanded to 30+ countries across Africa, Southeast Asia, Middle East, and Latin America with dedicated regional service centers.

2018

Next-Generation Platform Launch

Introduction of the X-series platform with Cummins partnership, bringing advanced powertrain technology to commercial vehicle operations.

2020

500+ Service Stations Milestone

Global service network reaches 500+ stations, providing comprehensive after-sales support, genuine parts, and technical assistance worldwide.

2023

New Energy Vehicle Launch

Introduction of the i9 electric truck series, marking SAGMOTO's commitment to sustainable transportation and zero-emission commercial vehicles.

2025

50-Country Global Presence

Export footprint expanded to 50+ countries with 7 production bases, establishing SAGMOTO as a truly global commercial vehicle brand.
