New Energy Vehicles

Zero-Emission Electric Trucks for a Sustainable Future

New Energy Vehicle Applications

SAGMOTO new energy vehicles represent the future of commercial transportation. Our electric truck lineup features advanced integrated electric drive axle technology, fast charging capability, and brake energy recovery systems. From urban logistics to specialized municipal operations, SAGMOTO EVs deliver zero-emission performance without compromising on reliability and durability. With a working range of up to 430km and support for 120KW fast charging, our electric trucks are ready for the demands of modern fleet operations.

Electric Light Truck

The SAGMOTO i9 electric light truck is purpose-built for urban logistics and last-mile delivery. Featuring an integrated electric drive axle with highly compact structure, it delivers superior efficiency and extended range. With a working range of 430km and 120KW fast charging that achieves full charge in just 65 minutes, the i9 minimizes downtime and maximizes fleet productivity. Brake energy recovery technology further reduces energy consumption by approximately 15%, making it one of the most cost-effective electric commercial vehicles in its class.

  • Integrated electric drive axle for higher efficiency and increased range
  • 430km working range with 120KW fast charging (65 minutes to full)
  • Brake energy recovery technology reduces consumption by ~15%
  • Zero tailpipe emissions ideal for urban low-emission zones

Electric Special Vehicle

SAGMOTO extends its new energy expertise into specialized applications with the i5 electric compressor vehicle. Designed for municipal and industrial operations, these electric special vehicles combine zero-emission performance with the versatility needed for specialized tasks. The electric platform provides quiet operation ideal for urban environments, reduced maintenance costs compared to diesel equivalents, and compliance with increasingly stringent emission regulations worldwide.

  • Electric chassis platform adaptable for various specialized upper bodies
  • Quiet operation ideal for municipal and residential applications
  • Significantly reduced maintenance costs vs. diesel equivalents
  • Meets global zero-emission vehicle mandates and regulations
