Your Trusted Commercial Vehicle Partner

SAGMOTO is a premier commercial vehicle brand under Shaanxi Automobile Group, dedicated to designing and manufacturing high-quality trucks that meet the diverse needs of global transportation, construction, mining, and logistics industries. With decades of engineering excellence, advanced manufacturing facilities, and a commitment to innovation, SAGMOTO delivers vehicles that combine power, reliability, and cost-effectiveness. Our comprehensive product lineup spans from light-duty to heavy-duty trucks, including tractors, dump trucks, cargo trucks, special purpose vehicles, mixer trucks, and new energy vehicles.
