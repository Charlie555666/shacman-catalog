SAGMOTO is a premier commercial vehicle brand under Shaanxi Automobile Group, dedicated to designing and manufacturing high-quality trucks that meet the diverse needs of global transportation, construction, mining, and logistics industries. With decades of engineering excellence, advanced manufacturing facilities, and a commitment to innovation, SAGMOTO delivers vehicles that combine power, reliability, and cost-effectiveness.
