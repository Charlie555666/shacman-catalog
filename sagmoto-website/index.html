Who We Are

SAG Commercial Vehicle Company — A core subsidiary of Shaanxi Automobile Holding Group

Dual-driven — Creating a new brand for the international market

Founded in 1968, Shaanxi Automobile Holding Group Company currently employs 32,000 staff with total assets of 34.8 billion CNY. SAG Commercial Vehicle Company, located in Baoji City, is a core subsidiary dedicated to the international market.

SAGMOTO is a new brand initiative by Shaanxi Automobile Holding Group aimed at the international market. We provide SAG commercial vehicles and parts for worldwide customers with customized solutions, meeting the diverse needs of the local market.
