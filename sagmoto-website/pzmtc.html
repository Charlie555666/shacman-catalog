Off-road Tractor

Flexible, lightweight and powerful

After years of research and development, SAGMOTO's off-road tractor has now formed two major series and three fuel versions (diesel, natural gas, and pure electric), with a product layout of more than 20 derivative models. It can adapt well to the operational requirements of ports, logistics zones, and steel plants, etc.

Streamlined exterior design

The skeletal welding skin cabin can effectively protect the safety of the driver in collisions and high-altitude falls. An escape door is set on the driver's side for easy emergency escape. The all-round glass window and the large flat rearview mirror can reduce blind spots effectively.
