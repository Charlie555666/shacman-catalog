SAG Commercial Vehicle Company won the "National Machinery Industry Quality Award" for 2021-2022.

The National Machinery Industry Quality Award is the highest award for quality in the machinery industry approved by the State Council of China. It is organised by China Association for Quality Management in Machinery Industry and is assessed and rewarded in accordance with the Performance Excellence Evaluation Guidelines. The award is selected once a year. It is the highest honour in terms of quality granted to machinery industry enterprises that have implemented performance excellence management in China and have made remarkable achievements in terms of quality, economic and social responsibility.
